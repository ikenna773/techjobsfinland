The Challenge

A problem on both sides of the world

Two groups with exactly what the other needs — and no reliable structure connecting them.

🏢

For Finnish companies

Finland has world-class tech companies. But with a population of 5.6 million, the domestic talent pool for specialised engineering roles simply cannot keep pace with demand.

Backend engineers, cloud specialists, data engineers, AI and ML roles — these positions sit open for months. Sometimes longer.

The talent exists. It just isn't here yet.

🌍

For international engineers

Talented engineers across the world are looking for structured European opportunities. Finland — with its innovative companies, genuine quality of life, and English-speaking tech sector — is a real option.

But the path is unclear. Hiring culture, visa processes, salary expectations, relocation realities — the information is scattered or simply missing.

The opportunity is real. The route to it just needs to be built.

Our Process

A structured hiring bridge — not a job board

Tech Jobs Finland is a curated process that prepares both sides properly — and connects them when the fit is right.

01

Education first

We work with international tech communities to educate engineers on Finnish hiring culture, salary benchmarks, visa realities, and what Finnish companies actually look for. Informed candidates make better hires.

02

Curated shortlists

We don't send companies a stack of CVs. We build a shortlist of engineers who are technically strong, genuinely interested in Finland, and properly prepared for the process.

03

Honest introductions

We make introductions only when the fit looks real. Finnish companies get candidates who are ready. Engineers get a fair shot at a market they understand. Both sides know what to expect.

Who We Work With

Built for two audiences

We work on both sides of the equation — and we're honest about who we're the right fit for.

Finnish startups & scaleups

Companies that have a real need and are ready to act on it

  • Engineering roles that have been hard to fill domestically
  • Open to international hiring but want a structured process
  • Value curated pipelines over volume of CVs
  • Ready to support a relocation hire through the permit process

We focus on companies where the hiring manager is engaged and the role is real. We don't work with companies who are vaguely curious.

International engineers

Engineers who are serious about Finland — not just browsing

  • Mid-level or senior: backend, cloud, DevOps, data, or AI/ML
  • Genuinely considering relocation to Finland
  • Willing to engage with the preparation process honestly
  • Connected through one of our partner communities

If you want a fast placement, we're probably not the right fit. If you want a real, well-supported route into the Finnish tech market — we are.

Why Finland

The honest case for building your career here

Not the tourist version. The one that actually helps you make a decision.

💰
€45k–90k+

Competitive Salaries

Mid to senior engineers earn between €45k and €90k+. Structured around the real cost of living, with strong public services reducing your out-of-pocket expenses.

🏔

Work Culture

Flat hierarchies. Direct communication. Genuine work-life balance — not just a talking point. Finnish companies expect ownership, and give you the space to exercise it.

🌐

English Is Enough

Most Finnish tech companies operate entirely in English. Finnish is useful for everyday life — it's not a barrier to getting hired in the tech sector.

📄

Manageable Visa

With a job offer and the right documents, Finland's permit process is structured and achievable. Finland targets 90-day processing for specialist tech roles.

🌿
#1

Quality of Life

Consistently ranked among the happiest countries in the world. Strong public services, safety, and a natural environment most cities can't offer.

🚀

World-Class Ecosystem

Wolt, Aiven, Varjo, ICEYE, RELEX Solutions — built from a country of 5.6 million people. Finland's output-to-population ratio is extraordinary.

Finland is not a consolation prize. It is a deliberate choice made by engineers who want to build something meaningful in a place that actually works.
